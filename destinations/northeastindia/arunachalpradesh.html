Arunachal Pradesh – The Land of the Rising Sun

Arunachal Pradesh, the easternmost state of India, is a land of pristine beauty, snow-capped mountains, ancient monasteries, and diverse tribal cultures. Often called the "Land of the Rising Sun," this Himalayan gem is perfect for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and spiritual travelers. Whether you want to explore the serene Tawang Monastery, trek through Ziro Valley, or witness the stunning landscapes of Sela Pass, Arunachal Pradesh offers an unforgettable travel experience.

Top Tourist Destinations in Arunachal Pradesh:
  • Tawang: Home to Tawang Monastery, Sela Pass, Bum La Pass, and the scenic Madhuri Lake.
  • Dirang: A hidden gem with Dirang Dzong, Sangti Valley, and hot springs.
  • Bomdila: A peaceful retreat featuring Bomdila Monastery, Eaglenest Wildlife Sanctuary, and Bomdila Viewpoint.
  • Bhalukpong: Gateway to Arunachal, known for Pakke Wildlife Sanctuary, Kameng River, and Tipi Orchid Research Centre.
  • Ziro Valley: A UNESCO Heritage Site, famous for its lush rice fields, Apatani tribal culture, and the famous Ziro Music Festival.
  • Itanagar: The capital city, home to Ita Fort, Ganga Lake, and the Jawaharlal Nehru State Museum.
Best Things to Do in Arunachal Pradesh:
  • Trek through the lush green Ziro Valley.
  • Experience snowfall at Sela Pass and Tawang.
  • Attend the famous Hornbill Festival or Ziro Music Festival.
  • Try authentic Arunachali cuisine – Thukpa, Momos, and Bamboo Shoot dishes.
  • Meet local tribes and experience their unique traditions.
Best Time to Visit:

March – June: Pleasant weather, ideal for sightseeing & trekking.
July – September: Lush greenery, best for photography (but expect heavy rainfall).
October – February: Best for snowfall, monastery visits, and winter festivals.

How to Reach Arunachal Pradesh?
  • By Air: Nearest airports – Lilabari (Assam), Tezpur (Assam), Guwahati.
  • By Train: Nearest railway station – Guwahati.
  • By Road: Well-connected via scenic highways from Assam.

Arunachal Pradesh Itinerary – 6 Days & 5 Nights

Day 1: Arrival in Guwahati & Transfer to Bhalukpong
  • Arrive in Guwahati and drive towards Bhalukpong.
  • Visit the Tipi Orchid Research Centre.
  • Enjoy riverside relaxation at Kameng River.
  • Overnight stay in Bhalukpong.
Day 2: Bhalukpong to Dirang
  • Visit Dirang Dzong – a 500-year-old fort.
  • Explore the stunning Sangti Valley for bird-watching.
  • Relax at the natural Hot Water Springs.
  • Overnight stay in Dirang.
Day 3: Dirang to Tawang
  • Drive through the breathtaking Sela Pass.
  • Visit Madhuri Lake (Sangestar Tso).
  • Explore the famous Tawang Monastery.
  • Overnight stay in Tawang.
Day 4: Tawang & Bum La Pass Excursion
  • Visit Bum La Pass (Indo-China Border) with a permit.
  • Explore Pangang Teng Tso Lake.
  • Witness a stunning sunset at Gyangong Ani Gompa.
  • Overnight stay in Tawang.
Day 5: Tawang to Bomdila
  • Drive back to Bomdila.
  • Visit Bomdila Monastery and Bomdila Viewpoint.
  • Explore the Eaglenest Wildlife Sanctuary.
  • Overnight stay in Bomdila.
Day 6: Bomdila to Guwahati – Departure
  • Drive back to Guwahati.
  • Drop-off at Guwahati airport/railway station.
