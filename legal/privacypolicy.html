Privacy Policy

Welcome to The Holiday Affairs. Your privacy is important to us & this Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you use our Website and services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and billing details when you make inquiries or bookings.
  • Payment Information: If you book through us, we collect payment details via secure third-party payment processors.
  • Travel Preferences: Destination choices, trip duration, and activity preferences.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device details through cookies and analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process bookings, reservations, and payments.
  • Send travel recommendations, promotional offers, and newsletters (only if you opt-in).
  • Improve our website experience and personalize content.
  • Comply with legal and security requirements.

3. How The Holiday Affairs Shares Your Information

  • Service Providers: Hotels, airlines, tour operators, and third-party booking platforms for trip arrangements.
  • Payment Processors: Secure third-party payment gateways for transactions.
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law, fraud prevention, or security purposes.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

  • Enhance your browsing experience.
  • Understand how visitors interact with our Website.
  • Provide personalized travel recommendations.

Managing Cookies: You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not function properly.

5. Data Security

    The Holiday Affairs takes reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or misuse. However, no online platform can guarantee 100% security but The Holiday Affairs will try its best to restore it.

6. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access & Update: You can request to access, modify, or delete your personal data.
  • Opt-Out: You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time.
  • Data Deletion: You may request us to delete your data unless we are required to retain it for legal reasons.

7. Changes to This Policy

    The Holiday Affairs may update this Privacy Policy from time to time and the changes will reflect on this website.
